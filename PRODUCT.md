# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Gamers, families, and friends in and around Patna City looking for a premium place to play (PS4/PS5, pool, snooker, table tennis, carrom) and eat (burgers, pizza, momos, shakes). Primary audience: local young adults and families deciding where to spend a few hours. Secondary audience: out-of-town visitors and event/group bookings.

## Product Purpose

Pocket Play Cafe is Patna City's premium gaming lounge and cafe. The site exists to show what's on offer, build appetite for the space and food, and convert visitors into walk-ins and bookings. Success looks like a visitor who arrives knowing the games, the menu, and the vibe before they step in.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: View menu & pricing
- Secondary CTA: Call / book a visit
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "Patna City's premium gaming lounge and cafe — play more, pay less, eat better."
- Belief ladder: This is a real, premium spot (not a dingy arcade) → It has the games and food I want → The prices are fair → I should go this week.
- Proof on hand: on-site photos, the kitchen menu, the games list, location/contact details. Collect testimonials and event photos into `.impeccable/assets/proof/` later.

## Brand Personality

Warm, local, welcoming — a neighborhood spot that happens to look premium. Friendly first, luxe second. Confident without being cold.

## Anti-references

Not a cold corporate chain, not a cluttered generic "SaaS landing page", not a low-effort template with Inter-everywhere and purple gradients, not gray-on-gray text. Avoid AI-slop tells: side-tab accent borders, gradient text on headings, nested cards, bounce/elastic easing.

## Design Principles

- Show the space, don't just describe it — lead with real photos and the games list.
- Friendly clarity beats cold luxury; warmth in copy and imagery, polish in execution.
- One obvious next step per screen (menu & pricing first).
- Local pride: speak to Patna City directly, use real place names and prices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Ensure body text meets contrast on the dark background, support reduced-motion, and keep touch targets large enough for mobile ordering/booking.
